MUSIXMATCH OFFICIAL LYRICS GUIDELINES (Web Summary)
Source: https://community.musixmatch.com/guidelines?lng=en

=== 1. TRANSCRIBE ===
- Listen to the whole song; transcribe all vocal content yourself (no copy-paste).
- Include extra-lyrical vocals: producer tags, backing vocals, live interjections.
- NO multipliers: repeat every line in full (never "x2" or "(X5)").
- #INSTRUMENTAL: use when 15+ consecutive seconds without lyrics (see Tag Structure).
- Expletives: transcribe as heard; use hyphen for audio-censored words (f-), never asterisk censoring.
- Do NOT transcribe: structure/artist labels, sound effects, descriptions.
- Vocalizations: include in moderation when stylistically/rhythmically significant.
- Use original scripts for non-Latin languages; follow Romanization policy when applicable.
- Sensitive content (hate speech, slurs, incitement, etc.): flag with community before saving.

=== 2. FORMAT ===
- Structure sections; max 10 lines per section.
- Capitalize first letter of each line and proper nouns; no erratic caps or ALL CAPS emphasis.
- Numbers: numerals except one through ten; phone numbers, dates, decades numerically.
- End-line punctuation: no commas or periods at line end; ? ! hyphens ellipses sparingly OK.
- Standardized slang: Ballin', 'Cause, Cuz, 'Em, Gon'/Gonna, I'ma, Outta, 'Til, Yo, Yo'.
- Direct speech: She said, "Do it like this"

=== 3. SYNC ===
- Sync when first letter of line is sung; tight sync, no breath gaps.
- Review line-by-line; minimum sync time = song duration.

=== 4. TAG STRUCTURE ===
Tags: #INTRO #VERSE #PRE-CHORUS #CHORUS #HOOK #BRIDGE #OUTRO
- Tag each section after listening to whole song.
- #INSTRUMENTAL between structure tags only (not within a section, not at start/end).
- Empty line after #INSTRUMENTAL before next structure tag.

=== 5. TAG PERFORMERS (reference) ===
- Tag individual stage names, not band names.
- Misc: #fanchant #voice-over #backing vocalist #robotic vocal

=== 6. TRANSLATE (reference) ===
- Line-by-line; one translated line per source line.
- Keep tone; keep untranslatable brands/names as-is.
