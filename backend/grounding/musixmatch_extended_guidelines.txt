=== EXTENDED POLICY NOTES ===

STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY
- Every audible vocal must appear in order; never skip ad-libs that are part of the performance.
- Section tags reflect musical/lyrical shifts, not arbitrary line counts.
- When the same section type repeats (e.g., second chorus), number tags: [Verse 2], [Chorus 2].

FORMATTING DEPTH
- Parentheses for parallel backing vocals on the same rhythmic slot as lead.
- Lowercase parenthetical lines for non-lexical vocal hooks that carry melodic weight.
- Avoid stage directions, crowd labels, or credit lines in the lyric body.

INSTRUMENTAL HANDLING
- 15+ seconds without sung lyrics → #INSTRUMENTAL between two structure sections.
- Never open or close a song with #INSTRUMENTAL alone.

QUALITY BAR
- Prefer accuracy over speed; re-listen to ambiguous passages.
- Harmonize bracket tags [Chorus] with Musixmatch hash tags (#CHORUS) only when exporting to Studio format; for this app output bracket tags per project spec.

SENSITIVE CONTENT
- Escalate ambiguous slurs or hate-adjacent phrases; do not silently omit without policy review.
